## Ingredients

- 1 tbs chili powder
- 1 tbs ground cumin,
- 1 tbs garlic powder
- 1 tbs onion powder
- 2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tbs Aleppo pepper (optional, but recommended)



## Tools
- Measuring spoons
- Jar


## Directions

1. Literally just mix everything together and pour it into a container. 
2. Make some tacos.
